Piki Delivery App

Piki App was designed to provide a seamless shopping experience, catering to the needs of busy individuals who prefer convenience without compromising on quality. The app partners with local supermarkets, such as La Colonia, to offer a wide range of products, ensuring that customers receive the best available options.

Key Features of Piki App

  • User-Friendly Interface: The app's interface is intuitive and easy to navigate, making it simple for users to browse through various categories and select their desired items.
  • Piki Shoppers (Pikers): Dedicated Piki Shoppers handle the task of selecting and purchasing items on behalf of customers. These shoppers are trained to pick the best products, ensuring quality and freshness.
  • Reliable Delivery: Piki App guarantees timely delivery of groceries. In the event of any missing or damaged items, customers can easily report issues through the app or other communication channels such as WhatsApp, Messenger, and Instagram.
  • Cost-Effective Solutions: Delivery costs are kept minimal, thanks to strategic partnerships with supermarkets and an extensive network of delivery routes.
  • Promotions and Discounts: Piki App frequently offers promotions and discounts, providing additional value to its users.

My Role in Piki App

As the Director of Technology at Piki App, I led the innovation and development efforts, ensuring the application was built using the latest technologies and best practices. My responsibilities included:

  • Backend Development: Utilizing Python and Django to create robust and scalable backend services that handle user authentication, order processing, and real-time inventory management.
  • API Integration: Developing APIs to facilitate smooth communication between the frontend and backend systems, ensuring a seamless user experience.
  • Data Analysis: Implementing data analytics solutions to monitor user behavior, optimize inventory management, and improve service efficiency.
  • Team Collaboration: Working closely with frontend developers, designers, and project managers to align on project goals and deliverables.
