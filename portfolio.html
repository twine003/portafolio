Piki App

Piki App project focuses on simplifying the home delivery service, offering a platform that makes ordering quick and convenient. The application allows users to receive orders anywhere, pay with cash or card with no minimum purchase, place orders within seconds, and track their deliveries in real-time. Additionally, Piki App offers a variety of product categories available for order, including restaurants, beverages, bookstore items, convenience products, and local entrepreneur products under the 'Nica Emprende' category. The platform also facilitates registration and affiliation for local businesses as well as 'Pikers', who can increase their income by efficiently managing their deliveries.
